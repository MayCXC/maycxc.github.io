TUNGSKRAT

Range [EC70, ECEF]

Editor

Tungskrat is a constructed script, meant to be used as an approximately phonetic orthography for whatever purposes one may be needed for. It consists of 127 glyphs, including a blank space, 16 punctuation marks used for quotation and dialogue notation, 25 mathematical and logical operators, a base 36 positional number system, 47 letters, and 2 additional punctuation marks used to denote glottal stops and agglutination.
