GRAFLECT

Range [EC70, ECEF]

Editor, Pronunciations, IPA, Sample.

Graflect is a constructed and featural writing system with an alphabetic and phonemic orthography, meant to be used for whatever purposes one may be needed. It contains 127 glyphs, including a blank space, 16 punctuation marks used for quotation and dialogue notation, 25 mathematical and logical operators, a base 36 positional number system, 47 letters, and 2 additional punctuation marks used to denote glottal stops and agglutination.
